Courses We Offer

You can find in almost all the fields in the University. Engineering, IT and Social Sciences.

Intermediate

After finishing your High school you can choose your field from different options. There are plenty of options such as IT, Mathematics, Engineering and Social Sciences

Graduation

Have you finished your Studienkollege? Then you are at right place to study further. Choose the course according to your major in Studienkolleg. In Engineering faculty we offer Civil Engineering, Electrical Enginnering and Mechanical engineering.

Post Graduation

If you graduate one of these courses then you are good to go for post graduation. We offer the same post graduation courses in our university which are available for graduation.
